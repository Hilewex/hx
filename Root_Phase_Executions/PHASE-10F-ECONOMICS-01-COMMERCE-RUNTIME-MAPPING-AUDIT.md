## 6. Risk Assessment

- Revenue leakage risk: Order-to-settlement path platform commission ve creator margin calculation'i kullanmadigi icin supplier net order line total'e esitlenebilir (`services/settlement/src/settlement.ts:425`, `:431`).
- Double/manual accounting risk: Ledger append API durable ve idempotent olsa da economics owners tarafindan otomatik cagrilmadigi icin payment/order/settlement/payout truth ile ledger truth ayrisabilir (`services/finance/src/finance.ts:12`, `packages/persistence/src/finance-ledger.ts:126`).
- Refund exposure risk: Refund success settlement/payout rollback yaratmaz; finance impact manual API disinda bagli degil (`services/refund/src/refund.ts:315`, `:247`, `:251`; `services/finance/src/finance.ts:93`).
- Payout premature/incorrect payout risk: Payout item generation settlement status ve impact flags'e bakar, fakat settlement line economics foundation ve payable lifecycle yoktur (`services/payout/src/payout.ts:295`, `services/settlement/src/settlement.ts:437`).
- Creator/supplier dispute risk: Creator selected price ve supplier base/pool base snapshots checkout/order/settlement live chain'inde korunmuyor; order line only customer-facing price snapshot tasir (`services/order/src/order.ts:162`; `packages/contracts/src/order.ts:8`).
- Reward liability risk: Reward points cash-equivalent degil ve ledger cash entry false; redeemed reward refund clawback BLOCKED/REVERSAL_REQUIRED ile kalir, negative balance runtime yok (`services/customer-reward/src/customer-reward.ts:222`, `:231`).

## 7. Next Safe Sequence

1. Commerce economics event contract ve source correlation sabitle: payment captured, order created, order line economics snapshot, settlement calculated/finalized, payout prepared, refund impact recorded eventleri icin contract-only owner boundary.
2. Order line economics snapshot'i genislet: selected sale price, pool base price source, supplier id, creator store id, commercial product id, discount allocation refs; once read-only snapshot olarak.
3. Settlement calculation'i order orchestration'a kontrollu bagla: order line economics snapshot'tan supplier net, platform commission, creator margin ve discount sponsor impact uret; ledger/payable/payout false kalmaya devam etsin.
4. Finance ledger append orchestration'i owner-event bazli ekle: once payment capture ve settlement calculated entries; append-only idempotency ve source refs ile.
5. Payable lifecycle'i settlement'tan ayir: supplier/creator payable pending/held/released/reversed state modeli ve refund/risk hold source refs.
6. Refund rollback'i payable/settlement/ledger uzerinden idempotent reversal olarak ekle: once amount-based partial refund, sonra coupon sponsor reversal.
7. Payout preparation'i sadece released payable source'a bagla: settlement line direct source yerine payable source; risk/refund/finance correction hold korunarak.
8. Payout execution truth'u provider boundary ile netlestir: provider submitted, succeeded, failed, returned state'leri; paymentInstructionCreated/actualProviderPayoutPerformed flag tutarliligi.
9. Reward accounting'i finance disi point ledger olarak durable hale getir: pending/spendable/redeemed/reversed/blocked, negative balance/debt offset; cash ledger ile karistirmadan.
10. Coupon/campaign owner service'lerini durable kural kaynagina tasima: static foundation rule set yerine sponsor, eligibility, margin guard ve checkout snapshot truth.
